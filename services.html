Services — Full value-chain BD&L

We operate across every stage where value is created in pharmaceuticals: discovery, clinical development, regulatory, manufacturing, distribution and commercialization. Our approach blends strategic rigor with local knowledge and measurable KPIs. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

1. Deal sourcing & opportunity screening

Targeted scouting using clinical filters, IP landscape, and market-fit scoring. We prioritize deals with clinical plausibility and scalable commercial potential.

2. Diligence & valuation

Clinical-readout scenario modelling, payer & pricing sensitivity analysis, and risk-adjusted valuation to inform term sheets.

3. Licensing & commercial structures

We design risk-sharing structures — exclusive licensing, co-development, royalties, milestones — with a focus on aligning incentives across partners and markets.

4. Regulatory & market access partnerships

Country-by-country market access playbooks: regulatory pathways, health-technology assessment expectations, reimbursement interactions and time-to-market planning.

5. Commercialization & launch strategy

Go-to-market planning, distributor selection, and performance KPIs to ensure sustained adoption and payer alignment.

6. Portfolio optimization & divestment

Lifecycle assessment and prioritization, plus exit planning when assets are better matched to other partners.

We recommend an iterative, metrics-driven BD process (screen → validate → structure → execute → measure) to keep deal pipelines healthy and focused. For a framework aligned with industry best practice see BCG's "Six ways..." for building effective BD strategy. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
