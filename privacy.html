Privacy — Plain-language summary

Leocyte respects privacy and treats personal data carefully. This page gives a short summary and a legal appendix describing our practices under the LGPD (Brazil), GDPR (EU) and CCPA (US). A full editable privacy policy template is available in /_data/privacy-policy-template.txt.

What we collect and why

  • Contact info (name, email, organization) — to respond to requests and manage relationships.
  • Professional & company data — to evaluate suitability for deals and partnerships.
  • Uploaded documents (e.g., NDAs, term sheets) — to perform diligence and structure deals; stored only as required and with restricted access.
  • Cookies & analytics — minimal website telemetry to improve the site. Analytics run only after consent for analytics cookies.

How we protect data

We use role-based access controls, encryption at rest and in transit, and detailed access logs. For uploaded commercial documents we recommend encrypted file transfer (SFTP or encrypted links) and storing DPA-backed contracts before sharing sensitive data. See assets/README.md for security recommendations.

How long we keep information

We retain basic contact and contractual records for business and legal purposes (typically up to 7 years unless law requires otherwise). Analytical cookies are retained per your consent settings and for no longer than necessary.

Rights & requests

You can request access, correction, restriction, portability, or deletion of personal data. To exercise rights or lodge a complaint contact: AlloAllo@leocyte.com. We will respond as required by applicable law (LGPD/GDPR timelines typically within 30 days; may vary by jurisdiction).

How we handle uploaded documents / NDAs

Uploaded documents and NDAs are treated as confidential client material. We:

  • Store them on encrypted storage with restricted access.
  • Log access and retain a minimum necessary copy for diligence and contractual fulfilment.
  • Offer secure transfer methods on request (SFTP, password-protected links, or secure virtual datarooms).
  • Remove or return documents on request or at the conclusion of engagement, subject to legal retention requirements.

Legal appendix (LGPD, GDPR, CCPA) — key points

This appendix outlines lawful bases, rights, international transfers, and cookie practices in plain language.

Lawful bases

  • Contractual necessity: Processing necessary to perform agreements (e.g., diligence, contract formation).
  • Legitimate interests: For business communications, fraud prevention and ensuring lawful operations (balanced against rights and interests of data subjects).
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional analytics/marketing cookies.

Data subject rights

Data subjects have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, object and request portability. Requests may be submitted by email to AlloAllo@leocyte.com — we will verify identity and respond per applicable law.

Data Protection Officer (DPO) / Contact

Contact: AlloAllo@leocyte.com (DPO / data privacy contact — replace with your DPO details here).

International transfers

Where data is transferred internationally we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), adequacy decisions where available, or lawful derogations when permitted.

Cookies & analytics

We use essential cookies for site operation and no analytics until you consent. You may grant or withdraw analytics and marketing consents at any time via the cookie banner (see cookie-consent.js).

Retention & deletion

Retention periods are minimized and documented. Deletion requests are actioned unless retention is required by law or contract; for NDAs or documents related to closed deals a retention policy will be communicated in the contract.

Security incidents

We maintain an incident response plan and will notify affected individuals and supervisory authorities as required by LGPD/GDPR timelines.
