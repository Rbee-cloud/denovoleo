Privacy Policy

Transparent data protection practices in compliance with global privacy regulations.

Plain Language Summary

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This summary explains key points from our full policy:

What We Collect

Contact information, communication history, and website usage data to provide our services and improve your experience.

How We Use It

To communicate with you, provide requested services, and improve our offerings. We don't sell your personal data.

Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at any time to exercise your privacy rights.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

Comprehensive Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller Information

Leocyte acts as data controller for personal data collected through our website and business operations. Our Data Protection Officer can be contacted at: AlloAllo@leocyte.com

2. Lawful Bases for Processing

We process personal data under the following lawful bases as defined by GDPR, LGPD, and similar regulations:

  • Contractual necessity: Processing required to fulfill our services
  • Legitimate interests: Business operations and communication
  • Consent: For specific purposes like marketing communications
  • Legal obligations: Compliance with regulatory requirements

3. Your Data Rights

Under GDPR, LGPD, CCPA and other privacy laws, you have rights including:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Consent withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: AlloAllo@leocyte.com

4. International Data Transfers

As a global business, we may transfer personal data across borders. All international transfers utilize appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and implement supplementary measures where required.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes collected, including to meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Typical retention periods:

  • Business communications: 5 years after last contact
  • Contract data: 7 years post-contract termination
  • Website analytics: 26 months
  • Job applications: 2 years for future opportunities

6. Analytics and Cookies

We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand website usage while respecting your privacy. Our cookie consent tool provides granular control over:

  • Essential cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Help us improve user experience
  • Marketing cookies: Used for relevant communications

You can manage your preferences at any time through our cookie banner.

7. How We Handle Uploaded Documents & NDAs

When you share confidential documents, including NDAs and business proposals:

  • Documents are stored in encrypted systems with access controls
  • Access is restricted to personnel with legitimate business need
  • Confidentiality obligations extend to all employees and contractors
  • Documents are retained only as long as necessary for business purposes
  • Secure deletion processes are followed when retention periods expire

We implement additional technical and organizational measures for sensitive business information to maintain confidentiality and integrity.

8. Data Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including encryption, access controls, secure development practices, and regular security assessments to protect your personal data.

9. Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities within timeframes required by applicable law (72 hours under GDPR, as soon as possible under LGPD).

10. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries, data rights requests, or concerns:

Email: AlloAllo@leocyte.com
Response Time: Within 30 days as required by applicable regulations

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect legal, technical, or business developments. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or direct contact when appropriate.
