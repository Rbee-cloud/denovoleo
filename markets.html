Strategic Market Focus

Deep local expertise across high-growth pharmaceutical markets with unique regulatory landscapes and partnership opportunities.

India

Market Size: $25B+
Growth Rate: 9% CAGR
Key Opportunity: Generics manufacturing, biosimilars, digital health

Regulatory Landscape

Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO) oversight with evolving pathways for innovative therapies. Price controls apply to essential medicines under NLEM.

Partnership Opportunities

  • Contract development and manufacturing (CDMO)
  • Biosimilar development and commercialization
  • Local distribution partnerships
  • Digital health and telemedicine collaborations

Strategic Insight

India's production-linked incentive (PLI) scheme creates attractive opportunities for manufacturing partnerships, while growing middle-class demand drives innovative therapy adoption.

Brazil

Market Size: $30B+
Growth Rate: 7% CAGR
Key Opportunity: Universal healthcare access, specialty medicines

Regulatory Landscape

ANVISA regulation with health technology assessment by CONITEC. Complex pricing and reimbursement environment with federal and state-level purchasing.

Partnership Opportunities

  • Local manufacturing to access public tenders
  • Specialty product distribution partnerships
  • Clinical research collaborations
  • Public-private partnership models

Strategic Insight

Brazil's SUS (Sistema Único de Saúde) represents massive purchasing power but requires local presence and price competitiveness. Strategic partnerships can navigate price-volume tradeoffs.

Portugal

Market Size: $4B+
Growth Rate: 4% CAGR
Key Opportunity: EU gateway, clinical research hub

Regulatory Landscape

INFARMED regulation with EMA alignment. Price and reimbursement decisions influenced by HTA and budget impact assessments.

Partnership Opportunities

  • EU market entry strategy
  • Clinical trial site partnerships
  • Distribution and commercialization
  • Real-world evidence generation

Strategic Insight

Portugal offers efficient EU regulatory pathways and competitive clinical trial costs while serving as strategic gateway to Portuguese-speaking markets including Brazil and Angola.

Mexico

Market Size: $12B+
Growth Rate: 6% CAGR
Key Opportunity: Manufacturing for US market, growing private insurance

Regulatory Landscape

COFEPRIS regulation with US FDA alignment advantages. Segmented market with public sector (IMSS, ISSSTE) and growing private insurance coverage.

Partnership Opportunities

  • US-focused manufacturing partnerships
  • Private sector distribution
  • Specialty product commercialization
  • Nearshoring manufacturing strategies

Strategic Insight

Mexico's USMCA membership creates manufacturing advantages for US market access, while growing private insurance expands innovative therapy market potential beyond public sector limitations.

Colombia

Market Size: $5B+
Growth Rate: 8% CAGR
Key Opportunity: Regulatory sophistication, Andean region hub

Regulatory Landscape

INVIMA regulation with evolving HTA framework. Unified health system (SISBEN) with growing capacity for innovative therapy funding.

Partnership Opportunities

  • Andean region distribution hub
  • Market access strategy development
  • Public sector tender partnerships
  • Regional clinical research

Strategic Insight

Colombia's stable regulatory environment and strategic location make it ideal for Andean region expansion, with growing HTA sophistication enabling evidence-based market access strategies.
